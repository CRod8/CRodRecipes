This is the best salsa ever:


1 cup of cilantro
6 roasted tomatillos
2 roasted jalapenos
1 avacado
1 lime
1 Pinch Salt
1 Tbs Olive Oil

Preparation:

Husk and rinse tomatillos. Cut in half and place on baking sheet, skin up. Wash and halve jalapenos and place on backing sheet skin up. Place in oven an Broil High until skin blisters/blackens.
Chop cilantro, squeeze lime, peel and seed avacado.
Add all ingrediants to blender and salt to taste. Blend and Cool. Serve.

*This salsa is amazing for dip, or adding to meat or enchiladas. Pairs with and mexican dish.
